Remembering every acronym and abbreviation you come across can be hard

Human beings are inherently lazy. Instead of writing a long phrase, we love to abbreviate it! But all those acronyms can be hard to remember. Worse, sometimes the same acronym will mean a different thing in a different context.

Not to worry! Whether you need help keeping up with the latest texting abbreviations, or you are trying to master technical jargon, My Acronym Dictionary (MAD) is here to help!
